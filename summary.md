### Problem Statement

In many organizations, L1/L1.5 support teams spend considerable time manually reading Jira tickets and assigning them to the appropriate support teams. This repetitive process increases response time and can lead to incorrect ticket assignments.

### Objective

The objective of this project is to automate Jira ticket triaging using an Agentic AI network. The system analyzes the ticket, identifies the appropriate team and sub-team, and routes the ticket automatically. The selected sub-team agent also provides a short troubleshooting suggestion or solution.

### Benefits

* Automates repetitive L1/L1.5 ticket routing tasks.
* Reduces manual effort and human error.
* Improves ticket routing accuracy.
* Speeds up ticket assignment and resolution.
* Provides an initial solution from the appropriate support team.
* Easily extensible to support additional teams and domains.

### Future Enhancements

* Integration with Jira APIs for automatic ticket assignment.
* Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) using historical tickets and knowledge bases.
* Confidence scoring for ticket classification.
* Support for additional enterprise domains such as DevOps, Cloud, Networking, Security, and Database Administration.
